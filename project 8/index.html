Where and What is Bruj Al Arab?

The Burj Al Arab is a luxury hotel located in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It's recognized for its distinctive sail-shaped silhouette, making it one of the most iconic landmarks in the city.

When Was Bruj Al Arab Made?

Completed in 1999, the Burj Al Arab stands on an artificial island and is connected to the mainland by a private bridge. The hotel offers opulent suites, world-class dining, and unparalleled views of the Arabian Gulf.

What Is the Hotel's Rating?

The Burj Al Arab has earned a reputation for being one of the most luxurious hotels in the world, often described as a "seven-star" hotel due to its opulence and exceptional service, although this is not an official rating. The hotel's lavish amenities and unparalleled guest experience contribute to its elite status and acclaim.
