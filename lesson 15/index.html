Utah is home to some truly stunning hills and landscapes. One of the most unique and colorful areas is the Bentonite Hills, also known as the Rainbow Mountains. These hills are located just outside of Capitol Reef National Park and are famous for their vibrant, Mars-like appearance. The hills are composed of bentonite clay, which gives them their distinctive colors and textures. If you're planning a visit, it's best to go during the blue hour for the most stunning photographs. Just make sure you have a high clearance vehicle, as the roads can be challenging.
