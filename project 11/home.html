Australia

Australia is both a country and a continent, located in the Southern Hemisphere. It's known for its unique and diverse ecosystems, including the iconic Outback, lush rainforests, and stunning coastlines. The country is home to famous landmarks such as the Sydney Opera House, the Great Barrier Reef, and Uluru (Ayers Rock). Australia is rich in cultural heritage, with a history that includes ancient Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander cultures, as well as influences from European colonization. The major cities, such as Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, and Perth, are vibrant and multicultural, offering a mix of modern urban living and access to beautiful natural landscapes. Australians, often referred to as "Aussies," are known for their friendly and laid-back attitude. The country is also famous for its wildlife, including kangaroos, koalas, and unique bird species.
